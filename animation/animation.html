One thing that should be on your bucklist for traveling is getting to see beautiful farmland. It's a great way to experience something new and a way to appereicate nature better. This is a perfect trip for families with children to come see, it's a place with activites and educational purposes.

Blooming Sunflower!

Sunflowers are bright, yellow, tall flowers that come to life in the summer. The flower blooms in mid summer to early autumn in North America. The closest Sunflower fields to New York City are in Long island and New Jersey. When you take the drive there remember to bring a camera and take lots of pictures!
