⬅Traditional Foods in Cartagena

Ceviche

Ceviche is a popular dish on the coast of Colombia that's also enjoyed throughout the country. It's made of shrimp, cilantro, red onion, lime juice and pink sauce. This dish is refreshing and perfect to serve at family parties.

Empanadas

Colombian empanadas are full of flavor and served as an appetizer for a party or afternoon snack. They're made of crunchy cornmeal dough, filled with chicken and potato, beef and potato filling and many other choices.

Sancocho

Sancocho is the ultimate family comfort food for lunch or dinner. It's a hearty soup alomst like a stew made with beef, potatoes, yuca, corn and plantain. It can also be made with fish, chicken or pork. The soups sides are rice, avacado and sometimes banana.

Arepa de choclo

Arepa de choclo known as sweet corn cakes are made with sweet corn, masarepa, milk, butter and suagr. You can add butter and stuff them with cheese. This is eaten for breakfast with a cup of coffee or hot chocolate.
