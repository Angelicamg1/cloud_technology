Brooklyn Botanic Garden

Learn

Brooklyn Botanic Garden is a botanic garden that showcases different types of species of flowers and plants. The garden was founded more than a century ago and was created to ensure green space was kept during the development of buildings. The garden carries over 12,000 of kinds of plants. It's the perfect environment to help families and children connect with and learn about nature. There's a variety of plants blooming every month of the year during all seasons.
