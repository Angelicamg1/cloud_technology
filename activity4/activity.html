Discovering MoMA

The Museum of Modern Art displays famouse modern artwork from artists with diverse backgrounds and timelines. There's another location in Longisland city, Queens called MoMA PS1 that focuses on newer art piceces from new artists. It gets vistors from all around the world and brings people together to connect and understand art. We welcome and celebrate creativty, openness, cultures, social and political views or issues. This is a home to all artists and their ideas.
